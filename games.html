Are you a healthy eater?

Why not try your hand at this fun game about healthy eating! The aim is to get the alligator to eat all the healthy foods and avoid all the unhealthy ones. See if you can beat your friends high scores!

Are you ready to blast off?!

These Astronauts need your help to send their rocket into space! But the only way that it can get there is if you fuel up their rocket with healthy food and energy from physical exercise! Do you know your healthy foods from your unhealthy ones? And which activities are best for active young space explorers? If so, its T-10 until blast off and you're at the controls!

Make sure your parents help you with choosing the best options for your ship, it may be harder than you think!

How About this game?

Do you know the difference between food that are high in bad sugars and fats from those that are great for your health? Test your knowledge with this fun and educational game about sorting some common foods into their right Categories! Remember to ask your parents if you need help, even the best healthy eaters need help sometimes!
