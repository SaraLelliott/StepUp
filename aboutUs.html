About Us

The aim of Step Up is to provide information to both parents and children to lay out a framework to living a healthy and active lifestyle. With obesity being such a prevalent issue within Australia, we wish to give parents and children an easily accessible site that informs them how to prevent their children from becoming obese and sharing information on how to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

For the parents on this website we provide:
  • Health information and resources
  • Templates for weekly meal plans
  • A BMI calculator
  • Role models for your children
  • Ways to motivate your children into changing their lifestyles
  • Conversations with Children-hints and tips
For the children using this site we provide:
  • Alternatives to unhealthy foods i.e. eat the rainbow
  • ways to motivate changes in eating and exercises
  • Fun and interactive activities to gain information about healthy living
